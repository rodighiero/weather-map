Map Legend

Clusters

GRAY contours indicates areas' activity

H stands for an area of HIGH activity

L stands for an area of LOW activity

GRAY curved lines mark opposed areas

GRAY stands for subjects of debate

Actors

GRAY crosses idicate actors' position

By zooming, actors' name is revealed

RED stands for emerging elements

BLUE stands for disappearing elements

By clicling, actors' further info is also revealed
